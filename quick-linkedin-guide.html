Quick LinkedIn Connections Import Guide

Follow these simple steps to import your LinkedIn connections into BeeTagged

3-Step Process

Access your LinkedIn profile, download your data, and import it to BeeTagged

1
Visit LinkedIn Data Privacy Settings

Log in to your LinkedIn account and navigate to your data export page:

  1. Click on your profile picture in the top right
  2. Select Settings & Privacy from the dropdown
  3. Click on Data privacy in the left sidebar
  4. Under "How LinkedIn uses your data" section, find and click Get a copy of your data
Tip: You can also directly visit LinkedIn's Download Your Data page after logging in.
Go to LinkedIn Data Download Page
2
Request and Download Your Connections

On the data download page:

  1. Select Want something in particular? option
  2. Check the box for Connections
  3. Click Request archive
  4. LinkedIn will prepare your data (usually within minutes)
  5. Check your email for a download link from LinkedIn
  6. Download the ZIP file and extract the Connections.csv file
Important: The connections file contains valuable metadata like company names, job titles, and when you connected - all of which BeeTagged will use to generate tags.
3
Upload to BeeTagged

Now that you have your connections data:

  1. Go to BeeTagged's LinkedIn Import page
  2. Drag and drop your Connections.csv file or click to browse
  3. Click Upload Connections
  4. BeeTagged will automatically process your connections and create tags based on:
    • Company names
    • Job titles/positions
    • Locations
    • Industries
    • Connection dates
    • Any custom tags you've added in LinkedIn
Privacy Note: Your data is processed locally in your browser - we don't store your connections data on our servers.
