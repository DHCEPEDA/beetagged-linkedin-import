How to Export Your LinkedIn Connections

1

Export from LinkedIn

Sign in to LinkedIn, go to Settings & Privacy > Data privacy > Get a copy of your data.

Select "Connections" and request your archive.

2

Download the CSV

LinkedIn will email you when your export is ready (usually within minutes).

Download the ZIP file and extract the Connections.csv file.

3

Upload to BeeTagged

Upload the CSV file using the form below.

We'll automatically tag and organize your connections.
