BeeTagged Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BeeTagged ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile information: Information you add to your profile such as profile picture, job title, industry, and other details.
  • Contact information: When you import or manually add contacts, we collect information about those contacts including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and any tags or notes you assign.
  • Communications: Information you provide when you contact us for support or communicate with us in any way.

2.2 Information From Social Media

If you choose to link your account with social media platforms like Facebook or LinkedIn, we may collect information from these platforms including:

  • Profile information: Name, profile picture, email address, and other public profile information.
  • Connection information: A list of your connections, including their names and basic profile information.

We only access the information you authorize us to access through the permissions you grant when connecting your social media accounts.

2.3 Device and Usage Information

When you use our app, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • Device information: IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, and other technical information.
  • Usage information: How you use our app, including features you access, time spent, and other analytics data.
  • Log information: Error reports, performance data, and other system activity information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • To enable you to import, organize, and manage your contacts.
  • To personalize your experience and provide suggestions for organizing contacts.
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests.
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends.
  • To protect our services and users from fraudulent, malicious, or illegal activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information when you direct us to do so, such as when you choose to share information with other users within the app.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who require access to your information to perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, payment processing, and customer service.

4.3 Compliance with Laws

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

4.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of business assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4.5 Protection of Rights

We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding potential violations of our policies, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person.

5. Social Media Integration

5.1 Facebook

When you connect your Facebook account to BeeTagged, we request access to your public profile and email. We use this information to:

  • Create or link your BeeTagged account
  • Import your profile information
  • Optionally import your contacts (with your explicit permission)

BeeTagged only accesses the information you explicitly grant permission to access and does not post to Facebook on your behalf without your consent.

5.2 LinkedIn

When you connect your LinkedIn account, we request access to your basic profile and email address. We use this information to:

  • Create or link your BeeTagged account
  • Import your profile information
  • Optionally import your connections (with your explicit permission)

BeeTagged only accesses the information you explicitly grant permission to access and does not post to LinkedIn on your behalf without your consent.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will also retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

6.1 Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account, you can do so through the app settings. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information and data associated with your account, though some information may remain in archived or backup copies for a limited period of time as required by law.

6.2 Facebook Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data obtained through Facebook Login:

  1. You can remove BeeTagged from your Facebook App Settings at https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
  2. Once removed, Facebook will notify us to delete your data
  3. You will receive a confirmation code and URL to track the status of your deletion request
  4. Data deletion is typically completed within 30 days

Our data deletion process complies with Facebook's Platform Terms and data deletion requirements.

6.3 LinkedIn Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data obtained through LinkedIn:

  1. You can disconnect BeeTagged from your LinkedIn account through your LinkedIn Permitted Services settings
  2. Once disconnected, you can request deletion of your LinkedIn data through our app or by contacting us
  3. You will receive a confirmation code and URL to track the status of your deletion request
  4. This process typically completes within 30 days

Our data deletion process complies with LinkedIn's Digital Markets Act (DMA) requirements, including proper handling of Member Changelog and Snapshot data.

6.4 Direct Data Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of all your data from BeeTagged by:

  1. Using the account deletion option in our app settings
  2. Emailing us at support@beetagged.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"

Please include your name and the email address associated with your account in your request. We will process your request and provide you with a confirmation code and status URL to track the progress.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update certain information through your account settings.
  • Delete: You can request deletion of your account and personal information.
  • Social Media Connections: You can disconnect social media accounts at any time through your account settings.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our emails.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or electronic transmission is ever fully secure or error-free, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through the app prior to the changes becoming effective. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the app to stay informed about our information practices.
