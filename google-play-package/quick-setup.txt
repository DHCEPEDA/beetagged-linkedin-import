BEETAGGED - GOOGLE PLAY STORE QUICK SETUP
========================================

WHAT YOU NEED:
1. Google Play Console account ($25)
2. Android app files (being generated)
3. 30 minutes to complete setup

STEP-BY-STEP:
1. Go to play.google.com/console
2. Create developer account and pay $25 fee
3. Click "Create app"
   - Name: BeeTagged
   - Language: English
   - Type: App
   - Price: Free
4. Go to Testing > Internal testing
5. Click "Create new release"
6. Upload the app bundle file
7. Add your email as tester
8. Install on your Android phone

APP DETAILS FOR PLAY STORE:
- Description: Smart contact management with Facebook and LinkedIn integration
- Category: Productivity
- Permissions: Contacts, Internet
- Age rating: Everyone

WHAT BEETAGGED DOES:
- Reads your phone contacts
- Connects to Facebook and LinkedIn (with permission)
- Adds smart tags like work, education, location
- Makes contacts searchable by company, school, city
- Works offline after setup
