How to Test

Install the APK

Download the APK file and install it on your Android device. You might need to allow installation from unknown sources in your device settings.

Launch the App

Open the BeeTagged Test app from your app drawer.

Grant Permissions

When prompted, grant the app permission to access your contacts. This is required for the app to read your phone's contact list.

Sign in with Facebook

Tap the "Connect with Facebook" button to sign in with your Facebook account. This allows the app to match your contacts with your Facebook friends.

View Enhanced Contacts

Your contacts will now be displayed with additional information pulled from Facebook, such as workplace, education, hometown, and more.

Note: This is a test app designed to demonstrate the BeeTagged contact enhancement functionality. It connects to our server at https://beetagged-server.replit.app to process your contacts and Facebook data.

Alternative Testing Method

If you prefer not to install the app, you can test the API directly using cURL or any API testing tool:

curl -X POST https://beetagged-server.replit.app/api/contacts/enhance-with-facebook \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "contacts": [
      {"name": "John Smith", "email": "john@example.com"},
      {"name": "Sarah Johnson", "email": "sarah@example.com"}
    ],
    "facebookToken": "YOUR_FACEBOOK_ACCESS_TOKEN"
  }'

Privacy Policy

BeeTagged takes your privacy seriously. The test app:

  • Only reads contacts but doesn't modify them
  • Temporarily processes data on our secure server
  • Does not store your Facebook access token permanently
  • Does not share your data with third parties
